Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Kevin Guimaraes ("we", "us", or "our") operates the EasyEstimate: Fast Quotes! mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

EasyEstimate is designed with privacy in mind. All estimate and business data you enter is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

The only information collected is:

Purchase Information: If you subscribe to EasyEstimate Pro, your purchase is processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not receive your payment details. RevenueCat may collect anonymous usage data to verify subscription status.

Photo Library Access: If you are a Pro subscriber and choose to upload a business logo, the App requests access to your photo library solely to select the image. The image is stored locally on your device only.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

• Your name, email address, or contact information
• Your clients' information
• Your estimate or invoice data
• Location data
• Device identifiers or analytics
• Any data to third party advertising networks

Third Party Services

RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat's privacy policy can be found at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Apple App Store: Purchases are processed by Apple. Apple's privacy policy can be found at apple.com/privacy.

Data Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS secure storage. We have no access to your data.

Children's Privacy

EasyEstimate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.
